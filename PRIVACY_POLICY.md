# Privacy Policy for Open Worship App

**Effective Date:** October 17, 2025  
**Last Updated:** October 17, 2025

## Overview

Open Worship App ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our open-source worship service application.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data Storage
Our application operates primarily as a local desktop application and stores data locally on your device:

- **User Preferences**: Language settings, display preferences, and application configuration
- **Content Files**: Bible translations, lyrics, playlists, background images/videos/sounds, and worship documents that you import or create
- **Directory Paths**: File system paths you select for storing worship content
- **Application Settings**: Window positions, font sizes, color preferences, and other customization settings

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the application
- **Location Data**: We do not access or store your geographic location
- **Network Activity**: We do not monitor your internet browsing or network activity
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device specifications or hardware identifiers

## How We Use Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely to:

- Maintain your application preferences and settings
- Store and organize your worship content (Bibles, lyrics, media files)
- Provide functionality for worship service presentations
- Remember your selected directories and file paths
- Preserve your customization settings between app sessions

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers (except for optional Bible downloads)
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time
- Data is stored in standard file formats and directories you specify

### Bible Downloads (Optional)
When you choose to download Bible translations:
- Downloads occur from publicly available Bible databases
- We do not track which Bibles you download
- Downloaded content is stored locally on your device
- You can delete downloaded Bibles at any time

## Third-Party Services

### Bible Content
- Some Bible translations may be downloaded from third-party sources
- Each Bible translation may have its own copyright and usage terms
- We display copyright information for each Bible translation within the app
- Please respect the copyright and licensing terms of each Bible translation

### No Analytics or Tracking
- We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook tracking, or similar services
- We do not embed third-party tracking pixels or cookies
- The application does not connect to social media platforms automatically

## Your Control Over Data

You have complete control over your data:

### Data Access
- All your data is stored in folders you specify on your device
- You can access, copy, or backup your files at any time
- Settings are stored in standard configuration files

### Data Deletion
- Uninstalling the application removes the program files
- Your content files remain in the directories you specified unless manually deleted
- You can clear application settings through the app's interface
- You can manually delete any folders or files created by the application

### Data Portability
- Your worship content is stored in standard formats (JSON, images, videos, etc.)
- You can easily backup, transfer, or migrate your data
- No vendor lock-in - your data works with other applications

## Children's Privacy

Open Worship App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The application is designed for use in religious settings and does not target children specifically. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us.

## Open Source Transparency

As an open-source application:
- Our source code is publicly available for review
- You can verify our privacy practices by examining the code
- Community contributions are welcome and reviewed publicly
- No hidden data collection or tracking mechanisms

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:
- We will update the "Last Updated" date
- We will post the new policy in our code repository
- Significant changes will be highlighted in release notes
- Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes

## Data Breach Notification

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- We cannot access your data remotely
- We cannot experience a data breach of your personal information
- Your data security depends on your device security practices
- We recommend regular backups of your important worship content

## International Users

Open Worship App can be used worldwide:
- All data processing occurs locally on your device
- No data is transferred across international borders by our application
- Local data protection laws in your jurisdiction apply to your use
- You are responsible for compliance with local regulations regarding content

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union:
- We process data based on legitimate interests for application functionality
- All processing occurs locally on your device
- You have the right to access, rectify, and delete your data at any time
- You have the right to data portability (your data is already portable)
- You have the right to object to processing (by uninstalling the application)

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents:
- We do not sell personal information
- We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing
- You have the right to know what information is collected (listed above)
- You have the right to delete your information (by uninstalling or manually deleting files)
- You have the right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## Retention Policy

- Application settings: Retained until you clear them or uninstall the app
- Content files: Retained until you manually delete them
- Temporary files: Cleared when the application closes
- Cache files: May be cleared automatically or manually through app settings

## Security Measures

While we don't have access to your data, we recommend:
- Keep your operating system updated
- Use antivirus software
- Regularly backup your worship content
- Use strong passwords for your user account
- Be cautious when sharing your device with others
